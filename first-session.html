First Session

What to Expect

Everything you need to know before your first conversation with Dr. Gargee.

It is completely normal to feel nervous. You don't need to prepare anything special — just come as you are. There is no right way to feel, no perfect thing to say.

Before your session

  • Find a quiet, private space where you won't be interrupted
  • A stable internet connection for online sessions
  • Headphones if possible — they help you feel more present
  • Water nearby. Tissues nearby. No special preparation required.

Online sessions

Sessions are currently held online via Google Meet or Zoom. Platform TBC You will receive a link after booking confirmation. Ensure your camera and microphone work — though you're welcome to keep your camera off if that feels safer initially.

During the session

Dr. Gargee will begin by welcoming you and explaining how the session will flow. You share only what feels comfortable. Silence is welcome. Tears are welcome. Questions are welcome. The session typically lasts 60 minutes. Duration TBC

After the session

You may feel lighter, tired, emotional, or reflective — all of these are normal. Dr. Gargee may suggest simple practices to try before your next session. There is never pressure to book again immediately.

Confidentiality

What you share in session stays between you and Dr. Gargee, except where required by law or where there is risk of harm. This will be explained clearly in your first session.
