Grounding & Support

Free tools to help you breathe, centre, and find calm — right now.

When overwhelmed, name:

  1. 5 things you can see
  2. 4 things you can touch
  3. 3 things you can hear
  4. 2 things you can smell
  5. 1 thing you can taste

This brings attention to the present moment and away from spiralling thoughts.

A simple 5-minute body scan:

  1. Lie down or sit comfortably. Close your eyes if safe to do so.
  2. Bring attention to your feet. Notice any sensation without changing it.
  3. Slowly move attention up: legs, belly, chest, hands, shoulders, face.
  4. Where you notice tension, breathe into that area and soften on the exhale.
  5. When ready, open your eyes and notice how you feel.

If these tools helped, a session can help you go deeper.
