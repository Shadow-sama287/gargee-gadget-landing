For Parents

You're Not Alone in This

Support for your child — and for you, as a parent doing your best.

Parenting is one of life's most demanding roles. When your child struggles — with anxiety, anger, withdrawal, or behaviour you don't understand — it can feel lonely and frightening. Dr. Gargee offers a calm, non-judgmental space for both children and parents.

Signs your child may benefit from support

  • Persistent sadness, worry, or irritability that affects daily life
  • Sudden changes after divorce, loss, relocation, or bullying
  • Difficulty sleeping, eating, or concentrating at school
  • Withdrawal from friends, family, or activities they once enjoyed
  • Outbursts that feel disproportionate or hard to soothe

This list is not a diagnosis. If you're unsure, reaching out for a conversation is always okay.

What child healing sessions look like

Sessions are age-appropriate, gentle, and paced for the child. Dr. Gargee uses conversation, creative play, and exercises suited to the child's developmental stage. Parents are typically involved in the first meeting and receive guidance on supporting healing at home.

Parenting guidance vs. child sessions

Child Healing focuses on the child's emotional world with the child present. Parenting Guidance focuses on you — your strategies, your stress, your relationship with your child. Many families use both over time.
